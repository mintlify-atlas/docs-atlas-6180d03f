CCReward helps you calculate and compare credit card rewards across banks in India and Singapore. This guide walks you through your first calculation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app and select your region">
    Go to [ccreward.app](https://ccreward.app). On your first visit, CCReward attempts to detect your location automatically using Cloudflare's geo-detection.

    - If you are in **India** or **Singapore**, the region is set automatically.
    - If detection fails or you are visiting from another country, a region selection prompt appears. Choose **India** or **Singapore**.

    Your region is saved locally in your browser and used for all subsequent calculations. You can change it at any time from the region selector in the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in">
    CCReward supports two sign-in options:

    - **Google Sign-in** — Sign in with your Google account to sync your preferences.
    - **Anonymous** — Continue without an account. Your data stays local.

    Some features, such as saving your card portfolio and accessing the Best Card Finder, require a signed-in session.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the Reward Calculator">
    From the home page, navigate to the **Reward Calculator**. This is the core tool for calculating how many points, cashback, or miles a specific card earns on a transaction.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your bank, card, MCC, and amount">
    Fill in the calculator form:

    1. **Bank** — Choose your bank from the dropdown. Only banks available in your selected region are shown.
    2. **Card** — Select the specific credit card you hold.
    3. **MCC** — Optionally search for a Merchant Category Code by typing a merchant name (e.g. "Swiggy", "Amazon") or the MCC number. Type at least 2 characters to trigger results.
    4. **Amount** — Enter the transaction amount. You can select the currency if the spend is international.

    <Tip>
      Use the MCC search to find the right category before entering your amount. Searching by merchant name (e.g. "Grocery", "Netflix") surfaces known merchants alongside their MCC codes, so you can confirm the correct category. See [MCC Lookup](/features/mcc-lookup) for more.
    </Tip>

    Some cards prompt for additional inputs — for example, whether a transaction is domestic or international, or whether you hold a premium membership. Fill these in when they appear.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View your results">
    Click **Calculate** to see the reward breakdown for your card and transaction. Results show the reward type (points, cashback, or miles), the earn rate applied, and the total earned for the amount entered.

    Click **Clear** to reset the form and start a new calculation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
