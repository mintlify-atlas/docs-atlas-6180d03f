## Why CCReward?

Credit card reward structures are complex. Each bank applies different rules:

- Some cards give **accelerated rewards** for specific merchant categories
- International transactions may earn **different rates** than domestic ones
- Reward points have **different monetary values** depending on the redemption method
- Some cards exclude certain MCCs entirely from reward earning

CCReward solves this by maintaining up-to-date reward rules for every supported card and computing exact earnings — not just estimates.
