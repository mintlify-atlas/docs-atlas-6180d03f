CCReward supports credit cards from **15+ banks** across Singapore, covering local banks, foreign banks, and digital-only banks. When you select the Singapore region, base calculations use **SGD (Singapore Dollar)**, with full support for international spend in other currencies at each card's applicable foreign exchange earn rate.

<Note>
  The list of supported banks and cards is updated regularly as issuers launch new products and revise reward structures. If a card you hold is not yet listed, you can request it directly from within the app.
</Note>

## Currency and international spend

Base calculations for Singapore cards use **SGD (Singapore Dollar S$)**.

<Info>
  Many Singapore cards offer **elevated earn rates for overseas and online foreign-currency spend**. When you toggle "International spend" in the calculator, CCReward applies the card's applicable overseas earn rate — which is often 2–4× higher than the local rate on cards like DBS Altitude, HSBC TravelOne, and UOB PRVI Miles.
</Info>

## Miles-focused cards

Singapore has one of the most miles-centric credit card markets in the world. A large proportion of premium cards are optimised for earning air miles, primarily through:

- **Airline co-brands**: KrisFlyer UOB, AMEX KrisFlyer, DBS Vantage (KrisFlyer transfer)
- **Transferable points**: Cards that earn points convertible to multiple airline and hotel programmes (e.g., DBS Altitude, Citi PremierMiles, HSBC TravelOne, OCBC 90°N)
- **Fixed-value miles**: Cards that earn miles directly at a fixed rate per S$ spent

<Tip>
  Use CCReward's **Best Card** feature to compare the miles-per-dollar (mpd) yield across your portfolio for a given MCC. This is especially useful for high-spend categories like dining (MCC 5812) and overseas transactions.
</Tip>

### Transfer partners

Several Singapore cards allow points to be transferred to airline or hotel loyalty programmes. CCReward supports transfer partner calculations — select a card that earns transferable points and the calculator will show effective miles earn after the transfer ratio is applied.

## How rewards are calculated

CCReward uses **Merchant Category Codes (MCCs)** to determine the applicable earn rate for each transaction. Singapore card issuers frequently define category-specific earn rates tied to MCC ranges — for example, dining (MCC 5812), travel agencies (MCC 4722), or contactless/online transactions.

To calculate rewards:

1. Select your bank and card.
2. Optionally enter the MCC for your transaction.
3. Enter the spend amount in SGD (or a foreign currency for overseas spend).
4. Toggle "International spend" if the transaction is in a foreign currency.
5. Click **Calculate Rewards**.

## Report a missing bank or card

If your bank or a specific card variant is not listed, you can submit a request:

- **In-app**: Use the "Bank or Card Missing?" button in the app to send details directly.
- **Contributing guide**: See [Adding banks and cards](/contributing/adding-banks-cards) for instructions on submitting data via a pull request.

<Tip>
  When reporting a missing card, include the full card name, the bank name, the earn rate (miles per S$ or cashback percentage), any category accelerators, and the relevant transfer partners if applicable.
</Tip>
