CCReward supports credit cards from **23+ banks** across India, covering the full spectrum of private sector banks, public sector banks, foreign banks, and fintech issuers. When you select the India region, all reward calculations are performed in **INR (Indian Rupee)**, and MCC-based rules are applied according to each bank's India-specific reward programme.

<Note>
  The list of supported banks and cards is updated regularly as new cards are launched and existing reward structures change. If a card you hold is not listed, you can request it directly from within the app.
</Note>

## Currency

All reward calculations for the India region use **INR (Indian Rupee ₹)** as the base currency.

<Info>
  CCReward also supports the **international spend** option for India cards. When you toggle international spend, the calculator applies the card's foreign transaction earn rate and any surcharge rules defined by the issuer.
</Info>

## How rewards are calculated

CCReward uses **Merchant Category Codes (MCCs)** to determine which earn rate applies to a transaction. Many India cards offer accelerated points or cashback for specific MCC ranges — for example, dining (MCC 5812), fuel (MCC 5541), or online shopping (MCC 5411).

To calculate rewards:

1. Select your bank and card.
2. Optionally enter the MCC for your transaction.
3. Enter the spend amount in INR.
4. Toggle "International spend" if the transaction is in a foreign currency.
5. Click **Calculate Rewards**.

## Report a missing bank or card

If your bank or a specific card variant is not listed, you can submit a request:

- **In-app**: Use the "Bank or Card Missing?" button in the app to send details directly.
- **Contributing guide**: See [Adding banks and cards](/contributing/adding-banks-cards) for instructions on submitting data via a pull request.

<Tip>
  When reporting a missing card, include the full card name, the bank name, and the reward programme details (earn rate per ₹ spent, category accelerators, and redemption options). This speeds up the review process.
</Tip>
